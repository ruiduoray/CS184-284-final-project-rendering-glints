Glint Rendering

Team Member: Weili Liu, Frank Cai, Xuantong Liu, Ray Gong

Overview

The goal of our project is to apply glint rendering to the path tracer in assignment 3. We looked at several papers aimed at solving this problem and decided to employ the high-resolution normal mapping strategy. We have essentially divided the project into two major parts: texture/normal mapping and high-resolution glint normal map generation.
